About the Temple

JMKDSS Naki Katra is a sacred place devoted to Maa Kali, offering a tranquil space for worship and spiritual connection. Located in Naki Katra, Bidhuna, Auraiya, the temple is a beacon of divine energy.

History

Established by local devotees of Maa Kali, the temple has long served as a center of devotion and religious ceremonies, preserving the cultural and spiritual fabric of the community.
